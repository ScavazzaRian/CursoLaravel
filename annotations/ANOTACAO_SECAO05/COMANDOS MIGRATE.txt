COMANDOS MIGRATE

Status  -> "php artisan migrate:status"  -> Verifica os status da migrations
Reset   -> "php artisan migrate:reset"   -> Reverte todas as migrations, executa o Down
Refresh -> "php artisan migrate:refresh" -> Ele reverte todas as migrations e executa o migrate
Fresh   -> "php artisan migrate:fresh"   -> Dropa o banco e realiza o migrate para recriar
